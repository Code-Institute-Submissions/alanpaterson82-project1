Benefits Of Yoga

Increased Flexibility

Increased flexibility makes it easier to move your joints in a normal direction with less effort

Back Pain Relief

A combination of movements encourages relaxation and increased flexibility of the joints and muscles of the back

Weight Loss

Yoga can help you burn calories, as well as increase your muscle mass and tone

Improved Mental Health

Yoga releases feel good chemicals which helps reduce anxiety and improve mood
