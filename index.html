Benefits Of Yoga

Increased Flexibility

Increased flexibility makes it easier to move your joints in a normal direction with less effort. It can help increase flexibility in your core muscles as well as the muscles in your back, chest, glutes, and legs

Back Pain Relief

A combination of movements encourages relaxation and increased flexibility of the joints and muscles of the back. For lower back pain, yoga can be especially helpful to the muscles that support the back and spine

Weight Loss

Yoga can help you burn calories, as well as increase your muscle mass and tone. It can also increase your heart rate, improve sleep, and reduce stress— all of which helps with weight loss

Improved Mental Health

Yoga releases feel good chemicals which helps reduce anxiety and improve mood. With its emphasis on breathing practices and medita­tion, it is of great benefit if you are looking to centre the mind and find calm
